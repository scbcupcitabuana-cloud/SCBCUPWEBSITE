GENERAL RULES

  • No bringing alcohol, cigarettes, e-cigarettes, or vapes inside the venue.
  • No drugs or illegal substances of any kind.
  • No weapons or dangerous items.
  • Respect all players, officials, staff, and volunteers.
  • No physical or verbal harassment.
  • No fighting, pushing, or unsafe behaviour.
  • Fans must not enter the field/court unless permitted.
  • No throwing objects onto the field/court.
  • Team benches are only for registered players and coaches.
  • Keep all areas clean. Dispose trash properly.
  • Follow all instructions from staff and security.
  • Any damage to venue property must be reported immediately.
  • Loud or aggressive fan behaviour may result in removal from the venue.
  • Cheering is encouraged. Taunting, insults, or racist remarks are not allowed.
  • All participants must wear their event IDs/badges.
  • Lost-and-found items must be submitted to the committee booth.
  • The committee has the right to remove anyone who violates the rules.
  • Decisions by referees and committees are final and cannot be argued.
