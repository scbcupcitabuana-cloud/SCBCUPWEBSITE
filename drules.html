MODERN DANCE RULES

  • Every team must pay a registration fee of Rp. 300.000 to be recognized as participants in the SCB Cup Modern Dance competition, along with an additional walkout fee of Rp. 100.000.
  • Every team must consist of at least 5 participants and a maximum of 10 participants and 1 accompanying person.
  • All team members must present their Student ID Card or a photocopy of their Birth Certificate during the Technical Meeting.
  • Each team member must be aged not more than 21 years old.
  • Every team must perform their dance with a minimum duration of 3 minutes and a maximum of 5 minutes.
  • The accompanying music must not contain any explicit language and must be submitted to the committee via Google Drive no later than 3 days before the competition. Teams are also encouraged to bring a flash drive/USB containing their music on the event day.
  • Props and decorations are allowed, but each team is only given 5 minutes to prepare themselves and their props before performing. Each team must inform the committee about any props or decorations to be used at least 3 days before the competition.
  • Team members’ outfits must be modest and uniform. Clothing such as shorts, mini skirts, tank tops, transparent clothing, or revealing outfits are not allowed.
  • There will be 3 judges which will judge the performance based on these criteria:
    • Technique & Movement Uniformity
    • Creativity & Choreography
    • Stage Performance
    • Synchronization with Music
  • The First, Second, and Third place finishers will be awarded a trophy along with a cash prize.
  • Setiap tim harus membayar biaya pendaftaran sebesar Rp. 300.000 agar dapat diakui dalam kompetisi Modern Dance SCB CUP, serta tambahan biaya walkout sebesar Rp. 100.000.
  • Setiap tim terdiri dari minimal 5 orang dan maksimal 10 orang anggota dan 1 orang pendamping.
  • Semua anggota tim harus menunjukkan Kartu Tanda Siswa/Kartu Pelajar atau fotokopi Akte Kelahiran pada Technical Meeting.
  • Setiap anggota tim wajib berusia di bawah 21 tahun.
  • Setiap tim harus menampilkan tariannya dengan waktu minimal 3 menit, dan maksimal 5 menit.
  • Musik pengiring tidak boleh mengandung kata-kata yang eksplisit, dan akan dikirim ke panitia lewat Google Drive, paling lambat 3 hari sebelum kompetisi. Tim juga dipersilahkan membawa flashdisk/USB Drive berisi musik pengiring-nya di hari-H.
  • Properti/dekorasi diperbolehkan, tetapi setiap tim hanya diberi waktu 5 menit untuk mempersiapkan diri dan properti sebelum penampilan. Setiap tim wajib memberi tahu panitia kalau ada properti/dekorasi yang akan digunakan, setidaknya 3 hari sebelum kompetisi.
  • Pakaian anggota tim harus sopan dan seragam. Pakaian seperti celana pendek, rok mini, tank top, pakaian tembus pandang, atau pakaian terbuka dilarang dipakai.
