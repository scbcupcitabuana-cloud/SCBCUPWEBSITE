About SCB CUP

SCB Cup is a major annual event organized by the Sekolah Cita Buana Student Council, offering schools in Jakarta and its surrounding areas the opportunity to showcase their talents and skills in sports and hobbies, while also enjoying a variety of entertaining performances. This event has become a long-awaited tradition for both students and schools eager to participate through their teams in various competitions. Through this event, every individual is given the space to express themselves and show their full potential.
